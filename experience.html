What I Do

Business Strategy

Planning sets of actions and goals a company takes to develop and market its software products, aiming to achieve a competitive advantage in the market by aligning the software features and functionalities with the overall business objectives and customer needs, ultimately driving revenue and growth.

App Development

Process of designing, creating, testing, and deploying software applications (or "apps") for various platforms like mobile devices, desktops, or web browsers, essentially meaning the full lifecycle of building a functional software program to solve a specific user need.

Mobile App

Process of creating software applications specifically designed to run on mobile devices like smartphones and tablets, involving the design, coding, testing, and deployment of apps that can be accessed through mobile operating systems like iOS and Android; essentially, it's the practice of building apps for mobile users.

Design

Process of planning and conceptualizing how a software system will work. It's a critical step in the software development process.

SEO Optimization

Integrating Search Engine Optimization techniques into the design and development of a website or web application, aiming to improve its visibility and ranking on search engine results pages (SERPs) by making the content easily understood and accessible to search engines, ultimately driving more organic traffic to the site.

UX Consulting

I'm a dedicated expert in user experience (UX) design to advise and guide a software development team throughout the development process, ensuring the final product is user-friendly, intuitive, and meets the needs of its intended users by focusing on research, usability testing, and design optimization throughout the project lifecycle.
